## Limitations

- Transfer risk is inferred from genetic context, including plasmid replicon, IS flanking, and conjugation marker co-localisation. It is not demonstrated by conjugation assays.
- Conjugation markers are detected by nucleotide similarity, a coarse proxy. Relaxase typing in the field uses protein HMMs such as MOB-suite, CONJscan, or oriTfinder.
- The screening engine is ungapped. Hits with indels relative to the reference allele may score lower identity than with a gapped aligner such as BLAST.
- Allele-level names reflect the best database match, not definitive clinical typing.
- Reported gene sets depend on CARD version, thresholds, and the overlap-collapse setting.
- Missing genomes in scaled runs should be re-fetched or replaced before final publication-grade interpretation.
